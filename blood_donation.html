Blood Donation Activity

Blood Donation Activity

Several millions of units of blood shortage is there in India every year. In order to address this issue our organization has undertaken live blood donations activity since 2009.

We are happy that our organization has arranged about 1000+ units of blood so far since starting of the activity. Moreover we are arranging live blood donations to the needy to avoid so many undesirable practices that plague blood donations. By live blood donations we mean donating blood to the patient directly and not to a blood bank.

Every year we have been conducting blood grouping activity in Bapatla Engineering College campus with the kind cooperation of Bapatla Education Society institutions namely Bapatla Engineering College and Bapatla Pharmacy College.
