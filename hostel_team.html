Hostel Team

Hostel Team

We know the famous saying "Todays children are tomorrows citizens" but millions of children are away from their first step of education and working as a child labour or ending up as a street children. SAC strives to curb the child labour and educate the poor and street children, as a part of it we tie up with the other NGO such as Pratham and other Government bodies. With the help of them we are running an orphanage. From April,2016 Pratham stopped providing accomodation due to lack of funds. So we have suspended the Hostel program for 1 year. As soon as we complete the building construction we will resume the Hostel works
