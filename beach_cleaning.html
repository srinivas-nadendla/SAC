bech cleaning

bech cleaning

We all know that beach is treated as a center of recreation, collection of memories and ultimate beauty of nature.

Everyone will be glad to play with the waves and enjoy the nature with our fellow beings.

As time passed, our beaches are being greatly polluted which leads to natural calamities and threat to our aqua life.

We members of SAC came forward to keep our surroundings clean.


We have selected the SURYALANKA beach which is located 9km from Bapatla.

Once in two months we will conduct cleaning program with the help of our volunteers, nearby college students and social welfare organizations.

We conduct few awareness program to the visitors

We took the help from nearby shopkeepers and arranged few dustbins in the beach.
