OUR MISSION: ‘NO CHILD WORKING, EVERY CHILD IN SCHOOL & LEARNING WELL’

Suryodaya society for awakening community is a formal committee of students formed at Bapatla Engineering college to bring a small change in and around Bapatla to help the under preveliged children by providing their basic needs.

We are inspired by the quotes of Swami Vivekananda quote “Youth are not useless, they are used less”…, & Mother Theresa “Helping hands are better than praying lips”.., and we would like to utilize our valuable time in Nation Building.

SUCCESSFUL PROGRASS:

“My lip Program – A five day program which involved 120+ students from various parts of AP. Society development and educational improvement has been the prime focus in the meeting.A similar program was conducted in hyderabad like the above except for the part that the focus was on helping Slums.”

“Nayeethalem – A four day national wide program with main motto of Implementing Nayeethalem in all the educational institutions, held at Sevagram, Gujarat.”

“ Maa Uriki Randi & Haritha Vanam – – Programs held with the focus on including Agriculture in the education system, held at Vikas Vidhya Vanam, Vijayawada and also program held for making Gandhiji's Nayeethalem principles as a part of the present education, held at Haritha Vanam, Palavancha, Khammam.”

“ Beach Cleaning: As our moral responsibility in protecting our environment, we have started an initiative to Clean our Beach so that everyone enjoys the pure atmosphere. SO we conduct awareness camps and conduct this program once in every two months.”
