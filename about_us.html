About Us

about sac

SAC is a formal committee of students formed at Bapatla Engineering college with a motto ‘NO CHILD WORKING, EVERY CHILD IN SCHOOL & LEARNING WELL’ established by N.Venu Gopala Rao on 3rd march 2008.

It was officially registered as a social welfare organization.0n June 3rd 2011(Regd No: 171 0f 2011 at Narsaropet).

SAC is now running successfully with more than 500 volunteers in different places.

Our Works

Educational Classes,and conducting Science Exhibitions in government schools.

Residential Shelter Homes for children in vulnerable situations.

Rescue and survey operations for freeing working children.

Working closely with government on advocating the needs of vulnerable children (working children)

Creating Awareness among the citizens in villages about the importance of education.

Donating blood to people in need.

Plantation programs.

The government cannot bear the responsibility of educating numerous illiterate children, hence people like us have to take the responsibility of educating children, who are either close to poverty or living below the poverty line.

OUR MISSION

‘NO CHILD WORKING, EVERY CHILD IN SCHOOL & LEARNING WELL’

We are inspired by the quotes of Swami Vivekananda quote “Youth are not useless, they are used less”…, & Mother Theresa “Helping hands are better than praying lips”.., and we would like to utilize our valuable time in Nation Building.
