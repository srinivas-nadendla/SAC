Medical camps

Medical camps

Even after decade of freedom, India is struggling to provide health services to its people. With the positive attitude of helping the needy there are many Organizations in our country offering free medical camps


We render our support to volunteer camps conducted by

GBR Foundation

Gillalamudi Amma Trust

Dr Inaja Kumari

GBR foundation conducts a medical camp once in every two months.

They provide free medical examination/check-ups and provide medicines to the people suffering with Diabetes, BP, Paralysis, Fits and Breathlessness problem. More than 2000 peple attend and get benifited from every camp.

Gillalamudi Amma Trust & Inaja kumara conduct medical camps in villages near by Bapatla.


We have been part of these camps since 2011 and help them in

Checking their medical reports and guide them to the respective doctor

Conduct some basic tests such as BP & Sugar

Distributing medicines based on doctor’s prescription

Suggest them health tips and best practices

Managing the Crowd
